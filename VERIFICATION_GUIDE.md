# How to Get Verified - BusNStay

## For Drivers, Restaurants, Hotels, and Taxi Drivers

### Step 1: Sign Up
1. Go to **Auth** tab (bottom of home page)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Select your role:
   - 🚗 Delivery Rider
   - 🍽️ Restaurant Owner
   - 🏨 Hotel/Lodge Owner
   - 🚕 Taxi Driver
4. Fill in your details:
   - Full Name
   - Phone Number
   - Business Name (if applicable)
   - **Operating Station**: Select your town/station from dropdown
5. Create account with email and password

### Step 2: Access Verification (Two Ways)

#### Option A: From Navbar
1. Log in with your service provider account
2. Click **"Get Verified"** button (green) in navbar
3. Or on mobile: Open menu → **Get Verified**

#### Option B: Direct URL
- Navigate to `/verification` in the app

### Step 3: Complete Verification Form
1. Select your role again (Driver, Restaurant, Hotel, or Taxi Driver)
2. Fill in required information:
   - **Documents**: Upload business registration, ID, etc.
   - **Contact Info**: Phone, email
   - **Operating Details**: Address, hours, etc.
3. Click **Submit Verification**

### Step 4: Wait for Admin Approval
- Admin team reviews your submission
- You'll receive an email when approved
- Once approved, you can start accepting orders

## Troubleshooting

### Station Dropdown Not Opening?
✅ **Fixed in latest build**
- Reload the app
- Try selecting role again
- If still not working, check console for errors

### Verification Page Shows Error?
- Make sure you're logged in as a service provider (Driver, Restaurant, Hotel, or Taxi)
- Passengers cannot access verification
- Try logging out and logging back in

### Don't See "Get Verified" Button?
- You must be logged in as service provider role
- Check navbar on desktop or mobile menu on phone
- Or go directly to `/verification` URL

## Demo Verification

To test verification in **Demo Mode**:

1. **Login as Demo Rider**
   - Click "Demo Rider" button at bottom of Auth page
   
2. **Go to Get Verified**
   - Click "Get Verified" in navbar
   
3. **Fill Demo Verification**
   - All fields are optional in demo mode
   - Click Submit to see demo success
